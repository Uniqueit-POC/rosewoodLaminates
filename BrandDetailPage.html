Quality Assurance

Technical Specification

All RANBerry laminates meet or exceed requirements as per IS 2046:1995 · Type S, VGS

01.

Thickness Precision

Manufactured to a precise 1mm (±0.1) thickness for consistent, reliable layering across every application.

02.

Surface Wear Resistance

Withstands a minimum of 350 revolutions — built to handle daily friction without showing a scratch.

03.

Boiling Water Resistance

Resists mass and thickness change when immersed in boiling water, maintaining structural integrity in high-moisture environments.

04.

Dry Heat Resistance

Handles dry heat up to 180°C without warping, discolouring, or degrading — safe around hot appliances and surfaces.

05.

High-Temperature Dimensions Stability

Maintains its shape and dimensions under elevated temperatures — no swelling, no shrinking, no surprises.

Installation Guide

Direction of Use

Follow these steps for a perfect, long-lasting laminate installation every time.

1

Before using laminate please ensure that the surface of plywood and laminate are clean, dry and free of other contaminations.

2

Please well stir adhesive before use and don't add water in it. Also ensure that the applied adhesive is sufficient.

3

Once laminate is pasted on plywood surface, apply adequate pressure for perfect bonding. Use a roller press from centre of assembly towards the edge to remove air bubbles.

4

A gap of minimum 2 mm should be maintained between two laminates while pasting side by side.
