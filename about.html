Who We Are

About Rosewood Laminates

Our Story

Two Decades of Crafting
Premium Surfaces

Founded over two decades ago, Rosewood Laminates has grown from a single-brand manufacturer to a diversified group housing 7 distinct laminate brands — each with its own design philosophy and market positioning.

Our manufacturing facilities span Gujarat and Rajasthan, equipped with state-of-the-art production lines capable of delivering over 1,200 unique SKUs annually. Every laminate sheet that leaves our factory undergoes rigorous quality testing before it reaches your hands.

We serve architects, interior designers, contractors, and homeowners across 28+ Indian states through a robust dealer network — and we are expanding internationally.
