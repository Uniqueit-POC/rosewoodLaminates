Our Promise

Why Choose Rosewood Laminates?

Rosewood Laminates is a trusted name in premium decorative surfaces, bringing together seven distinct brands under one roof — each crafted to meet the diverse needs of modern interiors. From rich wood grains to sleek mirror finishes, every laminate in our portfolio is designed to transform spaces with lasting beauty and purpose.

Rooted in quality and driven by innovation, our laminates conform to IS 2046:1995 standards and are ISO 9001:2008 certified. Whether you're designing a home, a workspace, or a commercial environment, Rosewood delivers surfaces that are as dependable as they are beautiful.

Our Manufacturing

Where Quality Takes Shape

Precision-engineered at every step, our state-of-the-art facility is where raw material meets craftsmanship — producing laminates that define industry standards.

At Rosewood Laminates, every sheet that leaves our facility carries the promise of perfection. Our manufacturing plant is equipped with advanced high-pressure lamination technology, operating under strict quality control protocols at every stage — from raw material selection to final finish. With full compliance to IS 2046:1995 standards and ISO 9001:2008 certification, we ensure that each laminate delivers on strength, durability, and visual excellence. Built on decades of industry expertise, our production facility is designed to serve the evolving demands of architects, interior designers, and homeowners across India.
